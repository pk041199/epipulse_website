NCD Prevention & Management Support

EpiPulse provides comprehensive support for non-communicable disease (NCD) prevention and chronic disease management at community and health system levels.

Our Services

NCD Risk Screening & Identification

  • Community-based risk screening programs
  • Population risk stratification
  • Early detection protocols
  • Screening tool development and validation
  • Health system readiness assessment

Prevention Program Implementation

  • Lifestyle intervention design and delivery
  • Workplace health programs
  • School-based health promotion
  • Community awareness campaigns
  • Risk factor modification support

Focus Areas: - Hypertension prevention and control - Diabetes prevention and management - Cardiovascular disease prevention - Respiratory health - Obesity and nutrition-related NCDs

Chronic Disease Management Support

  • Patient education and empowerment programs
  • Medication adherence support systems
  • Self-management training
  • Complication prevention protocols
  • Care coordination frameworks

Health System Strengthening for NCDs

  • NCD program design and implementation
  • Clinical protocol development
  • Health worker training
  • Supply chain management for NCD medications
  • Monitoring and evaluation frameworks

Data Systems for NCD Surveillance

  • NCD registry development
  • Disease burden estimation
  • Program monitoring dashboards
  • Quality improvement tracking
  • Evidence-based program adaptation

Evidence-Based Tools

We use standardized: - WHO validated risk assessment tools - Evidence-based clinical guidelines - Best practice intervention models - Health technology assessment frameworks

Target Audiences

  • Health departments and programs
  • District and primary health centers
  • Workplace health initiatives
  • Community-based organizations
  • Patients and caregivers

Implementation Approach

  1. Needs Assessment: Understand local context and NCD burden
  2. Program Design: Evidence-based, context-appropriate solutions
  3. Capacity Building: Train health workers and community leaders
  4. Implementation: Field execution with monitoring
  5. Evaluation: Impact assessment and continuous improvement
