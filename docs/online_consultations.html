Online Consultations via Google Meet

EpiPulse provides secure, accessible healthcare consultations through Google Meet, allowing you to connect with healthcare professionals from home.

How Online Consultations Work

Before Your Consultation

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Find a quiet, private space
  • Have any relevant medical documents/reports ready
  • Join 5-10 minutes before the scheduled time

During Your Consultation

  • You’ll connect via a secure Google Meet video link
  • Discuss your health concerns with your healthcare provider
  • Ask questions and get personalized guidance
  • Receive recommendations and next steps

After Your Consultation

  • Get a digital prescription via email
  • Receive follow-up instructions
  • Get any referrals if needed
  • Have your consultation summary for reference

Technology Setup

Minimum Requirements

  • Device: Computer, tablet, or smartphone
  • Internet: Minimum 2 Mbps connection
  • Browser: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge (latest version)
  • Permissions: Camera and microphone access

No Special Software Needed

  • Google Meet works directly in your web browser
  • No need to install additional applications
  • Works on Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android

Before Your Consultation

Checklist

Internet Speed Test

Before your appointment, test your connection: 1. Visit speedtest.net 2. Run the test 3. Check if download speed is at least 2.5 Mbps 4. If slower, try: - Moving closer to WiFi router - Closing other apps using internet - Using wired connection if possible - Restarting your router

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

I Can’t Hear the Doctor - Check microphone is not muted (unmute button at bottom) - Check speaker volume - Try wearing headphones - Restart the meeting

Doctor Can’t Hear Me - Check microphone is working in Meet settings - Test microphone before appointment - Ensure no apps are blocking microphone - Try different microphone if available

Video Not Working - Allow camera permissions in browser - Check if webcam is blocked by other app - Try different browser - Restart computer

Poor Video Quality - Move closer to WiFi router - Close other apps using internet - Reduce video quality in Meet settings - Check for WiFi interference (move away from microwave, etc.)

Can’t Find Meeting Link - Check email spam/junk folder - Request link to be resent - Call us: [Phone number] - Check your booking confirmation page

Wrong Device Name Showing - Disconnect other devices - Update browser/app - Restart Google Meet - Log out and log back in

Lag/Delay in Video - Check internet speed - Close unnecessary browser tabs - Restart WiFi router - Move to stronger WiFi signal area

Privacy & Security

Your Data is Protected

  • Encryption: Google Meet uses end-to-end encryption
  • Privacy: Only you and the doctor can see/hear
  • Recording: Only with explicit consent
  • Data Storage: Secure servers with backup
  • HIPAA Compliant: Follows healthcare privacy standards
  • No Third-Party Access: Your data is not shared

What You Should Do

  • ✅ Join from a private location
  • ✅ Use a strong password (if set)
  • ✅ Report any security concerns immediately
  • ❌ Don’t share your meeting link with others
  • ❌ Don’t record without permission
  • ❌ Don’t join from shared/public devices without caution

Meeting Etiquette

Professional Behavior

Please Do: - ✅ Arrive 5-10 minutes early - ✅ Dress appropriately - ✅ Maintain eye contact (look at camera) - ✅ Speak clearly and directly - ✅ Be respectful and honest about symptoms - ✅ Ask for clarifications - ✅ Take notes on advice

Please Don’t: - ❌ Multitask during consultation - ❌ Have distracting backgrounds - ❌ Use offensive language - ❌ Interrupt the doctor - ❌ Share personal information unnecessarily - ❌ Involve too many people in the consultation - ❌ Record without consent

After Your Consultation

Follow-up Steps

  1. Review Prescription
    • Read digital prescription carefully
    • Follow medication instructions
    • Note any precautions
  2. Follow Medical Advice
    • Implement lifestyle changes suggested
    • Take medications as prescribed
    • Complete any recommended tests
  3. Book Follow-up
    • If doctor recommended follow-up, book next appointment
    • Usually within 2-4 weeks
  4. Seek Emergency Help If:
    • Symptoms worsen significantly
    • New severe symptoms develop
    • Allergic reaction to medication
    • Call 108 or go to nearest hospital
  5. Share Feedback
    • Help us improve the service
    • Email feedback to: feedback@epipulse.org
    • Your input is valuable!

FAQ

Q: Is Google Meet secure for medical consultations? A: Yes. Google Meet uses industry-standard encryption and is HIPAA-compliant.

Q: Can someone else be present during my consultation? A: Family members can be present with your knowledge, but the consultation is confidential.

Q: What if I miss my appointment? A: Notify us within 24 hours to reschedule. Repeated no-shows may result in restrictions.

Q: Can I get a recorded copy of the consultation? A: Only if you request recording before the appointment. Both parties must consent.

Q: What payment methods do you accept? A: Currently cash/bank transfer. Contact us for payment options.

Q: Is the prescription valid legally? A: Yes, digital prescriptions from our licensed doctors are legally valid in India.

Q: Can I consult with a specific doctor? A: Request during booking. Subject to availability.

Q: What happens during technical failure? A: Doctor will attempt to reconnect. If unable, a callback will be arranged.
