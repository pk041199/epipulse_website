What We Offer

General Healthcare Services

  • Primary Care Consultations - General health check-ups and common illness management
  • Specialist Consultations - NCD care, maternal health, pediatric care, and more
  • Preventive Care - Health screenings and wellness consultations
  • Online Consultations - Secure video consultations via Google Meet

Service Categories

  • General OPD consultations
  • NCD (Non-Communicable Diseases) management
  • Maternal and child health services
  • Mental health and deaddiction support
  • Health education and counseling

How Appointments Work

Step 1: Book Online

  • Visit our booking system
  • Select your preferred service and time slot
  • Provide your contact and health information
  • Receive instant confirmation

Step 2: Prepare for Your Visit

  • Get appointment details via email/SMS
  • For online consultations: Receive Google Meet link
  • Prepare any relevant medical documents
  • Ensure stable internet connection (for online visits)

Step 3: Consultation

  • Join at scheduled time
  • Discuss health concerns with healthcare professional
  • Receive personalized advice and recommendations
  • Ask questions and get clarifications

Step 4: Follow-up

  • Receive digital prescription via email
  • Get follow-up instructions and referrals
  • Access consultation summary and notes

Online Consultation Details

Technology Requirements

  • Device: Computer, tablet, or smartphone
  • Internet: Minimum 2 Mbps stable connection
  • Browser: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge (latest version)
  • Permissions: Camera and microphone access

Before Your Online Consultation

  • Test your internet connection and device
  • Find a quiet, private space
  • Ensure adequate lighting
  • Have medical documents ready
  • Join 5-10 minutes early

During Your Consultation

  • Connect via secure Google Meet link
  • Professional video consultation
  • Confidential and secure communication
  • Real-time health guidance

Appointment Policies

Scheduling

  • Advance Booking: Book up to 30 days in advance
  • Same-day: Limited slots available
  • Emergency: Contact directly for urgent needs

Rescheduling & Cancellation

  • Free Cancellation: Up to 24 hours before appointment
  • Rescheduling: Contact us or use booking system
  • No-show Policy: Late cancellation fees may apply

Fees & Payment

  • Consultation Fees: ₹200 - ₹500 (varies by service)
  • Payment Methods: UPI, cards, bank transfer, cash
  • Insurance: Check with your provider for coverage

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
Click the booking link above and follow the online process.

What if I need to reschedule?
Contact us up to 24 hours before your appointment.

Are online consultations secure?
Yes, we use encrypted Google Meet for all online consultations.

Do I need to prepare anything?
Have your medical history and current symptoms ready.

What languages are supported?
English, Hindi, and regional languages as available.
