Resource Hub

Purpose

This page is the central academic and technical resource index for EpiPulse. It is designed to support research quality, reproducibility, ethics, and implementation readiness across all internal divisions.

Academic Objective

EpiPulse resources are developed to:

  • Strengthen protocol-to-publication research workflows
  • Improve evidence quality in public health decision-making
  • Build practical skills in epidemiology, analytics, and implementation
  • Standardize documentation, data governance, and reproducibility
  • Support collaborative work across research, program, and systems teams

Core Resource Collection

  • AI-assisted Data Cleaning Workflow
    Practical R workflow for de-identified AI-assisted data cleaning, typing, labeling, and export.

  • Community
    Discussion space for technical questions, peer support, and method exchange.

  • Transparency
    Legal structure, governance framework, and pre-launch operational status.

Resource Architecture by Internal Division

1) Research and Evidence Division

  • Protocol templates
  • Study design and methods notes
  • Evidence synthesis and review workflows
  • Manuscript and reporting guides

2) Public Health Implementation Division

  • Program design templates
  • Field implementation SOPs
  • Community intervention documentation tools
  • Operational learning notes

3) Collaboration and Partnerships Division

  • Collaboration framework notes
  • Partner onboarding documents
  • CSR-alignment concept notes
  • Joint implementation documentation standards

4) Accounts and Legal Compliance Division

  • Compliance checklist templates
  • Statutory reporting tracker formats
  • Donation policy and governance documentation
  • Basic audit-preparedness templates

5) Software and Systems Division

  • Data pipeline setup guides
  • Reproducible coding conventions
  • Version control and release notes templates
  • Data interoperability and export standards

6) Education and Fellowship Division

  • Learning modules for students and professionals
  • Workshop and mentorship materials
  • Fellowship and internship learning pathways
  • Competency tracking templates

7) Monitoring, Evaluation, Impact, and Ethics Division

  • Indicator frameworks
  • M&E toolkits
  • Ethics and data responsibility checklists
  • Impact reporting templates (to be activated post-launch)

8) Grants, Funding, and Resource Mobilization Division

  • Concept note templates
  • Grant narrative and budget drafting guides
  • Funding opportunity tracker formats
  • Donor communication structure templates

Recommended Path for New Users

  1. Start with the AI-assisted Data Cleaning Workflow
  2. Review legal and operational status on Transparency
  3. Use division-specific templates relevant to your workstream
  4. Discuss practical challenges in Community
  5. Archive outputs with clear versioning and data dictionaries
