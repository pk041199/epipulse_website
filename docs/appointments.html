## Cancel Your Appointment

### Cancellation Policy
- ✅ **Can cancel**: Up to 24 hours before appointment
- ❌ **Cannot cancel**: Less than 24 hours before appointment
- ⚠️ **Repeated cancellations**: May result in booking restrictions

### How to Cancel

#### Step 1: Notify Us

Thank you, [Your Name] [Your Phone] ```

Phone/WhatsApp: - Call or message: [Your phone number] - Say: “I want to cancel my appointment” - Mention your Appointment ID

Step 2: Confirmation

  • You’ll receive cancellation confirmation
  • No charge if cancelled within 24 hours
  • You can rebook anytime

No-Show Policy

What is a “No-Show”?

  • Booking an appointment but not joining the meeting
  • Not informing us of cancellation
  • Failure to join within 15 minutes of scheduled time

No-Show Consequences

First No-Show: - ⚠️ Warning email sent - Contact to discuss issues - Opportunity to explain

Second No-Show: - 🚫 Booking privileges suspended for 30 days - Must contact us to resume booking

Third No-Show: - ⛔ Account may be restricted - Must speak with management to reinstate

Avoid No-Shows

  • ✅ Mark appointment on your calendar
  • ✅ Set reminder 1 day before
  • ✅ Set reminder 1 hour before
  • ✅ Test internet morning of appointment
  • ✅ Complete all preparations before time
  • ✅ Notify us 24+ hours in advance if you need to cancel

Appointment Status Explained

Status Types

PENDING 🟡 - Your appointment request received - Awaiting final confirmation - We’ll contact you within 24 hours - Action: Confirm you can attend

CONFIRMED 🟢 - Appointment is confirmed - You have the Google Meet link - Doctor is assigned - Action: Prepare and join on time

IN PROGRESS 🔵 - Consultation is happening now - Doctor is online and waiting - Join the Google Meet meeting - Action: Click the Meet link

COMPLETED ✅ - Consultation finished - Prescription sent via email - Consultation notes forwarded - Action: Follow medical advice

CANCELLED ❌ - Appointment was cancelled - No charge if cancelled 24hrs before - You can rebook anytime - Action: Book new appointment

NO-SHOW 🚫 - You didn’t join the meeting - Doctor waited but consultation didn’t happen - May affect future bookings - Action: Contact us to explain

Payment & Billing

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Cash payment at center (if applicable)
  • Bank transfer (account details provided)
  • Digital payment (UPI - if available)

Billing Process

  1. Before Appointment: Fee confirmed (non-refundable if cancelled <24hrs)
  2. After Booking: Invoice/receipt sent
  3. At Consultation: Doctor confirms fee in meeting
  4. After Appointment: Receipt and summary sent

Refund Policy

  • ✅ Full refund if cancelled 24+ hours before
  • ❌ No refund if cancelled <24 hours or no-show
  • ⏱️ Refund processed within 5-7 business days

Special Situations

For First-Time Patients

  • Need to fill detailed health form
  • May take 10-15 minutes extra for orientation
  • Book accordingly with extra time

For Emergency Cases

  • DO NOT use appointment system
  • Call immediately: 108 (NDMA Emergency)
  • Go to nearest hospital
  • Telemedicine is not for emergencies

For Follow-up Consultations

  • Same doctor recommended for continuity
  • Can request same doctor during booking
  • Previous records reviewed automatically

For Corporate/Group Bookings

  • Bulk appointment discounts available
  • Contact: corporate@epipulse.org
  • Minimum 10 people required
  • Special time slots available

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I book multiple appointments at once? A: Yes, use the booking form multiple times or contact us for bulk booking.

Q: Can someone else attend my appointment? A: Family can be present, but consultation code is confidential to you.

Q: What if I’m late to join? A: Join within 15 minutes. After that, slot considered no-show.

Q: Can I reschedule multiple times? A: Yes, if done within 24 hours of each appointment.

Q: What if the doctor doesn’t show up? A: We’ll contact you within 15 minutes to reschedule. No charge.

Q: Can I get consultation in my language? A: Request during booking. Availability depends on doctor.

Q: Is my health information confidential? A: Yes, 100% confidential. Follows medical privacy laws.

Q: Can I share my appointment link with family? A: Not recommended. Your appointment is personal and confidential.
